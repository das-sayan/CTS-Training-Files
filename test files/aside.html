Dorothy together with her little black dog Toto, the Scarecrow, Tin Woodman and the Cowardly Lion travel to the Emerald City by following the yellow brick road. They all wanted to see the Wonderful Wizard of Oz. The Scarecrow wants to get a brain, the Tin Woodman a heart, and the Cowardly Lion, courage.
